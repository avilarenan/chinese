## Useful structures

**被**  
**bèi**  
passive marker (“to be …ed”)

**又**  
**yòu**  
again (past)

**还**  
**hái**  
still

**更**  
**gèng**  
more / even more

**比**  
**bǐ**  
than / compared with

**让**  
**ràng**  
to make / to cause someone to…

**越来越…**  
**yuèláiyuè...**  
more and more...

**一边……一边……**  
**yìbiān... yìbiān...**  
while doing A, also doing B

**我觉得……比较好**  
**wǒ juéde... bǐjiào hǎo**  
I think ... is better

# 2. Warm-up Sentences

Read these before translating.

**那个银行被黑客攻击了。**  
**nàge yínháng bèi hēikè gōngjī le.**  
That bank was attacked by hackers.

**今天所有的系统都关闭了。**  
**jīntiān suǒyǒu de xìtǒng dōu guānbì le.**  
Today all the systems were shut down.

**他们还不确定黑客怎么进去的。**  
**tāmen hái bù quèdìng hēikè zěnme jìnqù de.**  
They still are not sure how the hacker got in.

**这让我更烦。**  
**zhè ràng wǒ gèng fán.**  
This makes me even more irritated.

**今天比昨天热。**  
**jīntiān bǐ zuótiān rè.**  
Today is hotter than yesterday.

**你的汉语说得越来越好。**  
**nǐ de Hànyǔ shuō de yuèláiyuè hǎo.**  
Your Chinese is getting better and better.

# 4. Chinese → Portuguese

   **wǒ cóng zhèngquàn jiāoyìsuǒ shōují shùjù.**

2. **我替银行向证券交易所收集数据。**  
   **wǒ tì yínháng xiàng zhèngquàn jiāoyìsuǒ shōují shùjù.**

   **wǒ zài yúnduān shèjì néng shōují shùjù de xìtǒng.**

   **nàge yínháng bèi hēikè gōngjī le.**

   **nàge xìtǒng bèi pòhuài le.**

   **tāmen hái bù quèdìng hēikè zěnme jìnqù de.**

7. **黑客攻击又发生了。**  
   **hēikè gōngjī yòu fāshēng le.**

   **zhè ràng wǒ gèng fán.**

9. **今天比昨天热。**  
   **jīntiān bǐ zuótiān rè.**

10. **我们可以改上课时间吗？**  
    **wǒmen kěyǐ gǎi shàngkè shíjiān ma?**

11. **我觉得下课后吃比较好。**

    **nǐ de Hànyǔ shuō de yuèláiyuè hǎo.**
